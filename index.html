Our Love Story



It all began in 2012, when two young hearts met in middle school. A connection was formed, a bond that time could not break. Life took us on different paths, but fate had its own plans.



Years passed, yet the memories remained. In 2024, our paths intertwined once again, as if destiny had been patiently waiting for us to find our way back to each other. On May 13, Marko asked Yolina to be his forever, and she said yes, This time, we knew-it was always meant to be.



From childhood sweethearts to lifelong partners, our journey has led us here, to a new beginning. With hearts full of love and gratitude, we invite you to witness the next chapter of our story as we say “I do” on May 13, 2024.
